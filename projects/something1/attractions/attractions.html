My favourite places to see in egypt

The Pyramids

The Giza Pyramid complex , also called the Giza plateau in Greater Caira, Egypt that includes the Great Pyramid of Giza. the Pyramid of khafre. and the Pyramid of Menkaure. along with their associated Pyramid complexes and the Great sphinx of Giza

Khan El Khalili

Khan el-Khalili (Arabic: من الخليلي is a famous bazaar and souq (or souk) in the historic center of Cairo, Egypt. Established as a center of trade in the Mamluk era and named for one of its several historic caravanserais
